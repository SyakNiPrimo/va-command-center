      Social Media Management

      Post Rules

      • No hyphens or em dashes in captions.
      • Use a maximum of 5 hashtags.
      • For real estate posts, caption comes before image notes.
      • Full property address goes on the first line.
      • Property status starts in all caps.
      • Use “Exclusively listed by” with only the agent handle and @thejakobovgroup.
      • Add “EXP Realty and The Jakobov Group” at the beginning when needed.
      • Required hashtags: #arizona #RealEstate #TheJakobovGroup.
      • Create a short Canva video for listing posts when requested.
      • Export the Canva video as MP4 before posting.

      Daily Workflow Logic

      1. Open dashboard and copy the correct meeting message.
      2. Check Top 3 Must Finish Tasks.
      3. Clear urgent email, social, attendance, and admin items.
      4. Move waiting items to the correct waiting status.
      5. Mark completed work before ending the day.
      6. Use completed items to send Ari a short end of day report.
